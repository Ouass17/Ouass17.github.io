Material Project (Guessing Game) (Click here to change)

The rules of the game are simple, you will get a random theme (which will not be private) between four choices : someone famous (1), an animal (2), a movie (3), or a word (4).
After getting the theme, you have to choose something related to it, like a wolf if you got an animal, or like an athlete if your theme is someone famous. Then, you will proceed to make people guess what you are thinking by giving hints, or the definition if your theme is "a word".
Of course, you can't use something very obvious, like the name of the main character of the movie, or a famous nickname like "The King of Pop" if you were thinking of Michael Jackson.
